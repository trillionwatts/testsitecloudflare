Our Story

Trillion Watts is an unfinished story. I will continue writing it for years to come.I was eager to return home to Flint in 2021 after several years abroad in France. By chance, I ran into my old friend, the founder of GoodBoy Clothing Oaklin Mixon, who asked me to serve as his ghostwriter for a motivational book based on his life and experience as an entrepreneur. We discussed how we would publish the book and considered starting a publishing business and bringing on other partners. However, after a few weeks of discussions, Oaklin fell ill and, shortly after that, passed away.After mourning his death, I considered starting a publishing company on my own. I decided to focus on two things: education and motivation. I wanted to apply my background in policy review to help dispel myths and highlight trends that go underreported to give readers new perspectives. I registered Trillion Watts Publishing LLC in 2022. The goal: Provide a voice of hope amidst the clamor in modern media.The Weekly Positive Newsletter was started in 2024 (now Trillion Watts Magazine). Motivational books from different authors are in the works, and social media accounts feature essential trends. In addition, I provide businesses and organizations with storytelling content for promotional and commercial purposes.I am still writing our story. I would love to help you write yours.Myron Manns
